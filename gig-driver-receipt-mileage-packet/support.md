# Gig Driver Tax Packet Support

Gig Driver Tax Packet is a manual mileage, receipt, weekly packet, and quarterly PDF export app for iPhone.

## Contact

For support, privacy questions, refund-process help, or legal notices, email **[harshp.build@gmail.com](mailto:harshp.build@gmail.com)**.

## Billing

Gig Driver Tax Packet uses Apple In-App Purchase for subscriptions. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings -> Apple ID -> Subscriptions. Refund requests are handled by Apple at <https://reportaproblem.apple.com>.
